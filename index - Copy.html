Welcome to Lenz Handel, proudly owned by Ulrich Lenz. We’ve been supplying Germany with quality building materials, solar solutions, and electronics for generations.

Our Tradition

Founded by the Lenz family decades ago, Lenz Handel has grown from a small local shop into a trusted name across Germany. Under the leadership of Ulrich Lenz, we continue to honor our roots while embracing modern technology and sustainable practices.

Our family tradition is built on integrity, dedication to quality, and a deep respect for the environment. From premium building materials to cutting-edge solar panel installations and electronics, our mission remains the same: to help you create a future that’s as strong and enduring as our own legacy.
